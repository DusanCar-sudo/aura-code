# Ecclesia: Nikola Tesla

## Convergent findings
- Nikola Tesla was a prolific inventor who developed the alternating current (AC) power transmission system, which became the worldwide standard for electrical distribution and revolutionized energy grids.
- He made pivotal contributions to radio communication technology, with his patents and the Tesla coil recognised as foundational for wireless transmission.
- The Tesla coil – a resonant transformer producing high-voltage, high-frequency alternating current – was central to his experiments in wireless communication and energy transfer.
- His extensive patent portfolio (over 300 patents) covered groundbreaking innovations in electricity, radio, and related fields, and many of his patents remain relevant.
- Tesla’s work laid the groundwork for numerous modern technologies, including power systems, telecommunications, wireless charging, and renewable energy concepts.
- He is consistently regarded as a visionary inventor whose impact on science and technology is profound and enduring.
